Fourth Evolution Inc

The mission statement of Fourth Evolution Inc (4Ei) is 'Making distant hyptheticals real'; the name 'Fourth Evolution' is based on the definition of 'The Fourth Industrial Revolution'.  We want to create amazing products, both enterprise and commercial, find the best team members, and partner with motivated people/businesses who want solutions and technologies that help them implement their vision for the future.

We are inventing technologies and products in the following:

  • Intelligent Audio.
  • Immersive AI.
'Intelligent Audio' takes a new approach to audio and aims to replace the capture, transmission and reproduction of analog-based audio systems with ones that use Artificial Intelligence (AI) at its core.

'Immersive AI', means the integrated use of highly-graphical user experiences, such as Augmented Reality (AR), with AI.  Our technologies and products provide a new generation of user experience while leveraging AI technologies to perform the underlying tasks.
